# Scheduling — Product Scope

> **Module:** Scheduling (shared capability)  
> **Source:** [Product Scope](../product-scope.md)  
> **Last Updated:** March 8, 2026

---

Shared availability and slot management; powers booking and interview scheduling. **Uses Shifts** (see Cross-cutting) where availability is derived from or constrained by employee shifts.

## Current Features

- Shared availability and slot management
- Calendar sync
- **Booking** — Public booking pages for demos, consultations, services (used by Content & marketing)
- **Interview scheduling** — Schedule interviews with candidates, link to candidate record (used by HR)
