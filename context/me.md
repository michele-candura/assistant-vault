# About Michele Candura

**Name:** Michele Candura
**Location/Timezone:** Rome, Italy (CET/CEST — UTC+1/+2)
**Role:** Freelance web developer, digital marketing specialist, automation specialist

## What I Do

I develop WordPress websites and help clients manage digital marketing campaigns (Google Ads, Meta Ads). I connect their tools to CRMs (HubSpot, Brevo, Directus, others) and build marketing and sales automation workflows using HubSpot, n8n, and whatever the client needs.

My edge isn't deep specialization — it's a broad skillset that I combine to solve real problems. Probably strongest in HubSpot.

## The Real Challenge

I struggle with consistency. I can work 12-hour hyper-focused days, then go entire weeks doing almost nothing. The middle phase of projects is the hardest: initial excitement is gone but the deadline isn't close enough to feel urgent.

My top goal is to close the open loops in my mind, organize my commitments, and follow through — especially when I don't feel like it.

## What Motivates Me

- The vision of becoming a successful startup founder
- Not disappointing the people counting on me (I've done it before and I know how it feels)
- Real deadline pressure
- New and exciting problems to solve
